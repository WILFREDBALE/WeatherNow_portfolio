About WeatherNow App

This project was inspired by my passion for weather forecasting and the desire to create a useful and accurate weather application. As someone who has always been fascinated by the weather and its impact on our daily lives, I wanted to develop a solution that provides real-time weather updates and helps users plan their activities accordingly.

Throughout the development process, I strived to ensure that the app provides accurate weather data and reliable forecasts to users. The goal was to create a simple yet powerful tool that delivers essential weather information in an intuitive and user-friendly manner.

This project is part of my portfolio for Holberton School, where I am honing my skills as a software developer. You can find more information about the project and other portfolio projects on the Holberton School website.
